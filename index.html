About Me

I am currently an associate professor at School of Informatics, Xiamen University. I was also a visiting researcher at Visual Computing Group, Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) in 2017 and 2009~2011, respectively. I received my Ph.D. degree from State Key Laboratory of Computer-aided Design & Computer Graphics, Zhejiang University. My research interests include computer graphics and computer vision, especially lie in human-centered analysis, reconstruction, synthesis, and animation.
